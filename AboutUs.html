Umi no Mikaku (海の味覚)

"Umi no Mikaku" (海の味覚) is a Japanese phrase for "Flavors of the Ocean". The concept of "Umi no Mikaku" emphasizes the appreciation of the diverse and delicate tastes that come from the sea, including fish, shellfish, seaweed, and other marine life. It reflects the deep connection between Japanese culture and the bountiful resources of the ocean.

Our Story

Founded by Chef Koh and Chef Shao, Umi no Mikaku started as a small, intimate restaurant has blossomed into a culinary haven that continues to captivate and inspire. Over the years, we have evolved, not just as a restaurant but as a culinary destination.

Our relentless pursuit of excellence led us to establish a network of trusted suppliers in Japan, ensuring that we always have access to the freshest seasonal ingredients. This commitment to sourcing has allowed us to consistently surprise and delight our guests with unique omakase experiences.

Our Philosophy

At Umi no Mikaku, our philosophy is simple yet profound: to provide an exquisite omakase experience that transcends the boundaries of taste, presentation, and hospitality. It is our mission to immerse our guests in the rich tapestry of Japanese culinary traditions while crafting an intimate and unparalleled dining affair.

The Experience

With a limited number of seats, each meal at Umi no Mikaku is a personalized culinary masterpiece. We believe that dining should be an interactive and immersive experience, which is why our chefs engage with you directly, explaining each course and the inspiration behind it. This interaction allows us to tailor your meal to your preferences, ensuring a truly bespoke dining adventure.
