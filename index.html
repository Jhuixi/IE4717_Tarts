ABOUT US

Welcome to Umi no Mikaku, where culinary artistry meets Japanese tradition.

At Umi no Mikaku, we invite you on a gastronomic journey through the heart of Japan's rich culinary heritage. Nestled in the heart of the city, our intimate and exclusive omakase restaurant is a haven for discerning food enthusiasts seeking an unforgettable dining experience.

Our story begins with a passion for Japanese cuisine and an unwavering commitment to authenticity. Every aspect of our restaurant, from the elegant decor to the meticulously crafted menu, reflects our dedication to the art of omakase.

OUR CHEF

With over two decades of culinary experience, Chef Nakamura is a true artisan of Japanese cuisine. His journey in the world of food began at a young age, inspired by the delicate and intricate flavors of his homeland. From bustling Tokyo markets to quiet fishing villages, Chef Nakamura embarked on a culinary pilgrimage, learning from local masters and uncovering the secrets of the freshest ingredients.

With a lifetime dedicated to the art of Japanese cuisine, Chef Nakamura brings a symphony of flavors to Umi no Mikaku. "I believe that each dish is a story waiting to be told. It's a journey through time and place, a celebration of nature's bounty."

Join us for a culinary journey like no other, where Chef Nakamura transforms food into art, one plate at a time.
