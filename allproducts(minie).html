Our Story

Founded by Chef Koh and Chef Shao, Umi no Mikaku started as a small, intimate restaurant has blossomed into a culinary haven that continues to captivate and inspire. Over the years, we have evolved, not just as a restaurant but as a culinary destination.

Our relentless pursuit of excellence led us to establish a network of trusted suppliers in Japan, ensuring that we always have access to the freshest seasonal ingredients. This commitment to sourcing has allowed us to consistently surprise and delight our guests with unique omakase experiences.
